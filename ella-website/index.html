The Journey

From Cuba to the World

Born in Cuba, raised in Venezuela, and now a global advocate for Latin American art. My journey began with teaching water ballet and evolved into founding one of the most significant platforms for contemporary Latin American artists.

Through CIFO and my personal collection, I've worked to ensure that the voices of Latin American artists resonate in museums, galleries, and hearts worldwide—from the Metropolitan Museum of Art to the Tate Modern.
